CANDIDATE FAQs

Compensation - is there a difference in my compensation if I chose in-office vs remote?

Potentially yes. Facebook’s compensation philosophy is pay for performance and guided by the cost of labour of your chosen work location - we pay at the top of the market and total compensation includes a mix of base salary, bonus, and equity. Benefits by country here.

Example: If you moved, from 1 office location to another office location OR one country to another country, your compensation would be re-evaluated in the same manner that we approach remote vs office.

Compensation - will taxation differ on my income, base, bonus, RSU’s in Canada vs US?

Potentially yes. US, State and federal tax treatments differ from Canada provincial and federal regulations. We recommend seeking professional tax advice to make an informed decision.

Remote Setup - can I expense my home office setup and ongoing expenses, such as my internet?

You will be provided with all equipment required to perform your role. In addition, to start, a one-time taxable cash benefit of $2,000USD/2,300CAD. A yearly benefit to reimburse your internet up to $900USD (or local equivalent). In addition, an annual office supply stipend of $1,100USD/1,400CAD.

Office visits - I see an office would be near me, could I still visit?

Yes, however, remote workers should only visit an office occasionally for team meetings, critical business meetings, events, and occasional social visits. These discretionary visits should be limited to 2 days per month (excluding any required business travel into any office).
